FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We collaborate with clients across the globe (USA, UK, Australia, UAE, and more). We adapt to your time zones for meetings and ensure seamless communication via tools like Slack, Zoom, and WhatsApp.

Will I own the source code?

Absolutely. Upon final payment, 100% ownership of the Intellectual Property (IP) and source code is transferred to you. We do not hold your code hostage.

Do you provide post-launch maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to keep your website or app secure, updated, and running smoothly as your user base grows.

How do you handle payments?

We usually break payments into milestones (e.g., 30% upfront, 30% after design, 40% before launch). We accept international wire transfers, Wise, PayPal, and other major platforms.
